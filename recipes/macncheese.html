Mac N Cheese

Description

Delicious baked pasta and cheese sauce

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Salt
  • White Pepper
  • Milk
  • Yellow Mustard
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Cook pasta in boiling water. Drain. Set aside cooked pasta.
  3. Melt butter in large pot or saucepan over medium heat.
  4. Add sifted flour and whisk to create bechamel.
  5. Add salt and white pepper.
  6. Slowly add milk to create a roux, whisking constantly to prevent clumping of bechamel.
  7. As roux begins to bubble, add mustard and Worcestershire sauce, whisking to mix.
  8. Add cheddar cheese and stir to mix. Remove from heat.
  9. Add cooked pasta to cheese sauce and stir thoroughly to combine.
  10. Transfer pasta and cheese sauce to baking dish.
  11. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a crispy golden topping emerges.
  12. Remove from oven. Let sit for 5 minutes. Serve. Also reheats extremely well.
