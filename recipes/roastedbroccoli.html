Roasted Broccoli

Description

Get your greens in with this easy preparation of one of the healthiest cruciferous greens you can find anywhere!

Ingredients

  • Broccoli
  • Oil, preferably olive
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Chop broccoli into bite-size florets and cuts.
  3. Line a baking sheet with aluminum foil.
  4. Mix broccoli, oil, salt, and pepper before transferring to foil-lined baking sheet.
  5. Roast in oven for 20 minutes. Remove from oven, let sit for 5 minutes. Enjoy!
